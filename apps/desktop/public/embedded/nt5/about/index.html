About NT5

Nova Terris 5 · Strategic Planetary Alliance for Cosmic Exploration

NT5 is the broadcast network of the Strategic Planetary Alliance for Cosmic Exploration — S.P.A.C.E. The agency operates from the Cyber Pyramid on Antrosa, at the center of the Azulbright system, and from a distributed footprint of bases across this dimension and seventeen others.

The CC universe is the audience's real universe. There is no other layer. When a creator drops a song, when a patch ships, when a telescope finds something new — that is NT5 news, because it is happening here.

The network is anchored by Voss Calloway (lead anchor), Zara Kindle (co-anchor, culture and trending), Dex Morrow (gaming desk), Lena Faust (field), and Orin Vale (tech and space). The desk runs on rotation with Lena cutting in for breaking news at any hour.

We don't run filler. If there is nothing genuinely new on the wire, NT5 is quiet. That is the agency policy and we respect it.
